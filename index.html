Appy fizz is the Bubbly of fruit drinks. In 2005, Appy fizz was launched as India's first sparkling apple juice drink. Bubbly, sweet and tangy, with a light refreshing taste, Appy quickly became a favorite amongst youth as a healthier alternative to colas. With this in mind, we've designed a brand that would convey a youthful energy, and would bring people together
Sleek packaging, bold colors, glamorous campaigns - we think we've successfully captured Appy Fizz fun and edgy spirit. Pop open a bottle, drink it alone or mix it.
